  CROSS-DOMAIN OBSERVATION NOTES
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  The TAB dataset uses real European Court of Human Rights (ECHR) decisions.
  Models were trained exclusively on AI4Privacy synthetic text. Key differences:

  • Domain: AI4Privacy = short synthetic sentences; TAB = long legal paragraphs
  • Entity types: AI4Privacy has ~50 fine-grained types; TAB uses 8 coarse types
  • TAB DATETIME ≈ AI4Privacy DATE; TAB CODE ≈ AI4Privacy ID_NUMBER
  • TAB PERSON spans often include titles (Mr, Ms) which may confuse the masker
  • TAB entities tend to repeat across a document (coreference) — the first
    masking may prevent the filler from being consistent across mentions

  Masker recall < pipeline recall — some entities not masked but not leaked
  because the filler may rewrite text enough to obscure them naturally.
